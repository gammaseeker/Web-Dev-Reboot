Office Wiki Page

This is the storage room. Workers here use it to maintain and earn material.
This is an elevator. You can use it to go up and down to different floors and it saves you the trouble of walking.
This is the I.T. room. The workers here keep the computers up and running so others don't have to be powerless.
This is the office with desks. You use this place to keep your important things and sign the serious documents to earn $$.

Constraints

  1. You can only have 5 rooms per floor, including the elevator.
  2. The elevator must be placed on the rightmost location of each floor.
  3. If you have no elevator on a floor, you will be stuck there FOREVER.
  4. If you made a mistake, you are allowed to delete a room.
  5. When you are in at least $200 in debt it is the end of the road for you.
  6. Rooms and empolyees have effects on resources so use them wisely.
  7. Each employee has different characteristics. That means you need to place them in appropriate rooms.
    1. Normal people have no bonus effects on resources
    2. Productive people can earn you extra money
    3. Strong people can get you extra material
    4. Technical people can generate extra electricity
